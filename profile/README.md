Volunteer Connector is a platform that helps people find meaningful volunteer opportunities in their communities. We partner with Volunteer Centres across Canada to make it easy for nonprofits to find the help they need and for volunteers to discover causes they care about.

## What We Do

**For volunteers** — Search for opportunities that match your interests, skills, location, and schedule. Apply in minutes and manage everything in one place.

**For nonprofits and charities** — Post volunteer roles, manage applications, coordinate shifts, and grow your team of supporters.

**For Volunteer Centres** — A complete platform to serve the organizations and volunteers in your region, with the tools to manage memberships, communications, and reporting.

## Our Mission

We believe volunteering is one of the most powerful ways people can contribute to their communities. Our goal is to remove the barriers between willing volunteers and the organizations that need them.

## Get Involved

- Visit [volunteerconnector.org](https://volunteerconnector.org) to find opportunities near you
- If you represent a nonprofit, register your organization to start posting opportunities
- If you run a Volunteer Centre and want to bring Volunteer Connector to your region, get in touch
